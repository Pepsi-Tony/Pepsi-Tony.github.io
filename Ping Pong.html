Wangchuqin was born on May 11, 2000 in Jilin City, Jilin Province. He joined the Chinese national table tennis team in December 2015. On December 3, 2017, his partner Xue Fei won the men's doubles championship at the 2017 World Youth Championships. On July 21, 2018, he won the runner-up of the 2018 Korean Table Tennis Open Mixed Doubles. On August 28, Wang Chuqin helped the Chinese team win the men's table tennis team championship at the 2018 Jakarta Asian Games. On October 11, he defeated Tomokazu Zhangmoto 4-1 in the 2018 Buenos Aires Youth Olympic Men's Singles Final to win the men's table tennis championship at the 2018 Buenos Aires Youth Olympics. On December 9, 2019, he won the "Youth Sports Star" in the "Beijing Youth Model Â· Times Model" character selection. In July 2021, he was selected as a table tennis player of the Chinese sports delegation to the 2020 Tokyo Olympics. In September, he won the men's doubles championship of the 14th National Games. On November 29, 2021, Sun Yingsha, the mixed doubles partner of the Houston World Table Tennis Championships, won the mixed doubles gold medal.
